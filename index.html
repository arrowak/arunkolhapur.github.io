Experience

2013 - Now
Verse Innovation Pvt. Ltd., Bengaluru

I have been contributing to the Ads backend of the system since the time I joined Verse, rather known as Dailyhunt.
My initial 2 years here mostly were spent adapting to the corporate life and learning the system along with bug fixes and minor feature contributions.

I soon was left as a sole contributor for the most part of the system and hence had no other way but to step into the world of sleepless nights and overwhelming responsibilities. The existing system built on the open-source framework was so vast, confusing and overly engineered that I felt we could write a better, simple, scalable custom-built system to pave the way for the future.

In 2016, along with 2 other contributers, we initially scaled the system to handle 4k RPS (previously 1.2k) which bought us enough time to build a better system. We took the challenge to write the new system ground-up within 6 months, with the estimated ability to scale upto 30k RPS.

To our surprise, the same system has been holding up well even today, with 30+ developers and approx. 120k RPS. Although, there were multiple iterations to optimise the system at its pain points, most of the system is still intact and capable of more.

Further-on, we successfully integrated the real-time prediction engine, which contributed to the better conversion rates and better user experience.

In 2019, I contributed to design a highly scalable system that paved a way to extend the organisation's inventory to all it's partner sites and step into the business of real-time header bidding.

In 2021, we are looking to level-up the game and build a system for the next generation. With Dailyhunt's ever growing user base and monetisable inventory, even Josh is joining hands for the better future. The system we are planning is aimed at handling 10x more traffic with 3x reduced infra and ~6x faster response times.
